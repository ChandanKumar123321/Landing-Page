"POTATO"

Potatos are primarily composed of water (about 80%) and solids (about 20%). The solid portion primarily consists of carbohydrates (about 18%) and protein (about 2%). They are also a good source of vitamins and minerals, especially vitamin C and potassium

Key Charactristics:

  • Energy Source:Potatoes are a good source of carbohydrates, which are the body's primary source of energy.
  • Vitamins and Minerals:They contain a variety of vitamins and minerals, including vitamin C, vitamin B6, potassium, and manganese.
  • Fiber:Potatoes are a good source of dietary fiber, which aids digestion and promotes feelings of fullness.
  • Antioxidants:Potatoes contain antioxidants like vitamin C and certain phytochemicals that help protect the body from damage.
